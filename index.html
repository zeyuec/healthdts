Explanation

* Sleep Quality is catched from Jawbone stream data.
* Distance Quality is calculated by formula $quality = ($distance > 4800 ? 100 : ($distance/4800)*100)
* Mood Quality is calculated by formula $quality = ($moodPostive + $moodNegative + 20)/40.0*100;
* Final Score Formula is Average Sleep Quality * 0.5 + Average Mood Quality * 0.3 + Average Distance Quality * 0.2.
All the formulas are based on non-proof assumption and can be easily configured in the future.
